M'saken City

M'saken was founded at the beginning of the 8th century of the Hejire by (Shorfas) descendants of the prophet. Their ancestors migrated from Hijaz to Magreb in the period of the Idrissian dynasty.

The genesis of M'saken

The city would have been founded at the beginning of the VIII th century of the Hegira by a group of descendants of Hussein ibn Ali (grandson of the Prophet Muhammad (sws)).

City of mosques

M'saken is known as the city of mosques and the Koran. At the present time, we find in it no less than 80 mosques, as we find in it a number of writers and a group of memorizers of the Qur'an.

Historical sites

Middle Mosque, is the first mosque established in the town by the founders of the city of Masaken during the late first half of the 8th century AH (14 AD). It is located among the five palaces that make up the town of M'saken.
